OUR BELIEF

We as Commission Christian Church (CCC) are a biblically functioning church fulfilling the redemptive purposes of God. We believe just as Jesus gave His disciples the great Commission (Matt28:18-20), we are called and commissioned to go out into the world and make disciples of all nations. We are Jesus-centred, Spirit-filled people of God that seeks His Will in all we do. We are family-orientated and love spending time with like-minded people, building each other up in the faith through authentic community.

Matthew 28:19 “Go therefore and make disciples of all nations baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit”
